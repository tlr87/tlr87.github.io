How does life look through the c, passion and purpose' lens?
According to the text the life is broken up in to the 3 parts
1. Pleasure: Looking and experiencing the high. This is very hard to manage.
2. Passion: The rhythm and the place of your performance. When time flies by.
3. Higher Purpose:  Something which is grander than ourselves and has meaning to us.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?
