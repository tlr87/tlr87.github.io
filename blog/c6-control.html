When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?
When I am programing the trigger reactions trigger are:

  • Unable to understand the problem.
  • Working too long on a problem.
  • Not solving a problem.

A disproportionate emotional response comes from a fight or flight response or an emotional outburst. This comes from a lack of confidence or emotional history.
