I only fully started to embrace this concept at the very end of the process as I was running out of time. For example at the end I realised that my footer was showing in the middle of the page on the mobile. I decided to remove my footer form the mobile version. However I did have a shot at seeing how I could have my footer there. I am now after building this it’s makes me more since have a go, make mistakes but don’t worry.  
What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?
I had some experience. I know that meditation is this proses of look at your thought and emotions form an outside perception as you are focusing on the breath.  By focusing on the breath you try and be present in yourself.
What new things have you learnt about meditation?
One of the purpose of meditation is to make the mind uncluttered and stronger like riding a bike. Meditation is also the process of being kinder to yourself as reduce the nasty gossip in your head.
The analogy I liked was providing yourself with a loving grandmother figure inside of your mind.
Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?
If you need to react or move then try to keep focusing on the breath.  Look at thoughts as guests into the mind treat them with care and attention but let them go when they want to leave.  
Any other musings?
If You want you can have a Hello Kitty Hat on while mediating.
