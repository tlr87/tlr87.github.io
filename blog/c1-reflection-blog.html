First Blog!

So far the EDA experience has been a very reassuring and safe as I was nervous about getting back in to education since I have not studied since 2009.

Everyone has been really mindful, caring and very understanding. My impressions are that it’s a social learning environment that is as much about culture as it is about learning.

I want to become as much as a teacher as a learner. Above all I want to start making professional and social friends. With this in mind my expectations have not changed about EDA yet.

I am nervously optimistic about this leaning environment.
